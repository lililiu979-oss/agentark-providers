# Buyer Intake Template

Use this to make a need legible before matching it to a provider.

## Business Goal

What business result are you trying to achieve?

## Current Workflow

How is the work handled today?

## Bottleneck

Where does the process break, slow down, or cost too much?

## Required Inputs

What systems, documents, or data would the provider need?

## Expected Outputs

What does good completion look like?

## Review Boundary

What must remain human-approved?

## Timing

- one-time project
- recurring workflow
- pilot first, then expansion

## Constraints

- compliance
- accuracy sensitivity
- turnaround expectations
- tool or access limits
