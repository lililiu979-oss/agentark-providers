# Provider Positioning Checklist

Before applying, pressure-test your positioning:

- Can a buyer tell what workflow you own in one sentence?
- Can a buyer tell what outcome you help produce?
- Can a buyer see where human review still matters?
- Can a buyer see at least one credible proof artifact?
- Can a buyer understand what a first engagement would look like?

## If The Answer Is No

Tighten these first:

- offer naming
- workflow scope
- proof quality
- risk and escalation boundaries
- first-project packaging
