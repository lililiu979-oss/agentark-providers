# Example Provider Profile: Support Operations Agent

## Offer Name

Support triage and response operations agent for SaaS teams

## Buyer Problem

Support teams often waste time on repetitive ticket handling, weak categorization, and inconsistent escalation. This slows response time and makes QA hard.

## Workflow Owned

The agent handles:

- ticket classification
- priority tagging
- first-response drafting for approved categories
- knowledge-base grounded reply suggestions
- escalation routing for exceptions

## Human Review Boundary

A human reviews:

- account-sensitive cases
- refund or billing edge cases
- policy exceptions
- unresolved or low-confidence cases

## Inputs

- ticket feed
- help center content
- escalation rules
- tone and policy guidelines

## Outputs

- categorized tickets
- response drafts
- escalation flags
- QA notes on missed or risky cases

## Best-Fit Buyer

- growing SaaS support teams
- ops-heavy startups with rising ticket volume
- teams that want faster first response without losing control

## Proof To Show

- routing and escalation diagram
- sample response classes
- QA review examples
- exception handling policy
